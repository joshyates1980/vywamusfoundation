Clearing Unconscious Belief Patterns

  • August 4, 2012
  • unconscious belief patterns clearing choice-energy

Belief Patterns

When you are involved in a program of self-improvement, self-change, or self-transformation, regardless of the specific approach you choose to pursue, what you are essentially dealing with are the multitude of belief patterns held within the unconscious portion of your psyche.

Very little research has been done to expose these patterns, but what has been discovered is that wherever unconscious beliefs exist, there is a "freezing" of the dynamic vibration of the choice-energy which is the energy of creation available to all human beings, and that which creates all your experiences.

Indications of Denial

There has been so much denial played within the human psyche in regards to creation, manifestation, and "free-will choice" that most people are not even aware of the unconscious belief patterns which they energize all the time. It is not really that difficult to discern, however, where you are playing with such patterns, for the indications are visible to all "who have eyes to see."

What are these indications?

Anywhere in your life where you feel stuck, where you desire change and feel helpless, hopeless, or unmotivated.

Anywhere you believe someone or something else, other than you, is responsible for some experience you are having.

Anywhere you believe that the past or the future is somehow related to what is happening NOW.

Anywhere you feel anger, rage, fear, shame, guilt, judgement, victimization, doubt, or what is known as "low self-esteem."

There are many more and these are indications of areas in your psyche where you have "given up" in a very literal sense, your ability to consciously choose and create your own experience. If this sounds like a lot of territory, well it is, but it is not hopeless.

Using the manual created by Vywamus and the channels of The Vywamus Foundation, in conjunction with specific channeled belief patterns, you can begin to clear these "frozen spaces" in your psyche and make room for "new creations" in your experience.
